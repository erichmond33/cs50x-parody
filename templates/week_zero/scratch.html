My Project: Melon Game FEAT Spider Amon

Description

In this wonderful game, your goal is to use Spider Amon to eat all the watermelon or 100 of them.

This will be no easy task, and dare I say... just really really annoying because the game is not well made haha.

The Intro is quite long, so for the impatient click "Up Arrow" then "Space" to begin the game.

Controls

  • Move - Spider Amon will follow your mouse pointer

  • Toggle Sound - "1"

  • Grow Spurt - "Down Arrow"

  • Skip Intro - "Up Arrow" then "Space"
